    • Our Team

    At Antonio Francis Catering, we are dedicated to providing you with the best possible catering experience. Our team of experienced chefs and service staff are passionate about creating delicious and memorable meals.

    One of the things that sets us apart is our special traditional menu offerings. We know that many of our customers are looking for more than just your typical Western-style fare. That's why we offer a wide range of traditional dishes, including vinkubala, chicken feet, trotters, goat meat, and many more. These dishes are prepared with care and attention to detail, using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients.

    But don't worry if you're looking for more familiar fare - we also offer a wide selection of Western-style foods. From classic appetizers to hearty entrees, we have something for everyone. And if you have any dietary restrictions or preferences, just let us know - we are happy to accommodate special requests.
